## Quick Start Guide

Welcome to NoteDraw! This guide will walk you through creating your first visual note in just 5 minutes.

## Step 1: Prepare Your Content

Before you start, have your text content ready. This could be:

- Study notes from a lecture
- Key points from an article
- Meeting notes
- Any text you want to visualize

**Pro Tip**: For best results, keep your content focused on a single topic with 3-7 main points.

### Generation Mode

- **Detailed**: Creates comprehensive visual notes with more details
- **Compact**: Generates concise, focused visuals

## Step 3: Generate Your Visual Note

1. Paste your text into the input area
2. Select your preferred style and settings
3. Click "Generate"
4. Watch as our AI team works their magic!

The generation process typically takes 30-60 seconds depending on content length.

## Step 4: Review and Refine

Once generated, you can:

- **View** your visual notes in the gallery
- **Regenerate** any card you're not satisfied with
- **Customize** with your own prompt for specific changes
- **Download** in high resolution

## Tips for Better Results

### Content Tips

1. **Be Specific**: Clear, well-structured text produces better visuals
2. **Use Bullet Points**: Lists work great for visual representation
3. **Include Keywords**: Important terms will be highlighted
4. **Keep It Focused**: One topic per generation works best

### Style Tips

1. **Match the Occasion**: Use Business style for work, Cute for education
2. **Consider Your Audience**: Choose styles that resonate with viewers
3. **Experiment**: Try different styles with the same content

## Advanced Features

### URL Import

Don't want to copy-paste? Simply:
1. Click the URL button
2. Paste any article URL
3. We'll extract the content automatically

### File Upload

Upload your `.txt` or `.md` files directly:
1. Click Upload
2. Select your file
3. Content will be loaded automatically

### Custom Regeneration

Not happy with a specific card? Use custom prompts:
1. Click "Regenerate" on any card
2. Enter your specific instructions
3. Get a personalized result

## Troubleshooting

### Generation Taking Too Long?

- Check your internet connection
- Try with shorter content
- Refresh and try again

### Results Not What You Expected?

- Try a different visual style
- Use more specific content
- Add structure to your text (headings, bullet points)

## What's Next?

Now that you've created your first visual note:

1. **Share It**: Post to the [Gallery](/gallery) for others to see
2. **Save It**: All your projects are saved automatically
3. **Create More**: Experiment with different content and styles
